⚠️ Email Delivery Issue

Emails are being sent from the backend but not reaching your inbox. This is a common issue with email delivery.

🔍 Step 1: Check Spam/Junk Folder

Most common cause: Emails are being filtered as spam

Check these locations:

  • 📧 Gmail: Spam folder
  • 📧 Gmail: Promotions tab
  • 📧 Gmail: Updates tab
  • 📧 Gmail: Social tab
  • 📧 Gmail: All Mail folder

🔧 Step 2: Whitelist Sender Email

Add the sender email to your contacts to prevent future filtering:

From: Afterstate Events <afterstate.events@gmail.com>

How to whitelist:

  1. Add afterstate.events@gmail.com to your Gmail contacts
  2. Mark any existing emails from this address as "Not Spam"
  3. Create a filter to always deliver emails from this address

📊 Step 3: Check SendGrid Activity

Verify email delivery status in SendGrid dashboard:

SendGrid Dashboard Checks:

  • Go to SendGrid → Activity
  • Look for emails to ignaslapas.pm@gmail.com
  • Check delivery status (Delivered, Bounced, Blocked)
  • Look for any error messages

🧪 Step 4: Test with Different Email Address

Try sending to a different email address to isolate the issue:

Test Options:

  • Try a different Gmail account
  • Try a different email provider (Outlook, Yahoo)
  • Try a work email address
  • Check if the issue is specific to ignaslapas.pm@gmail.com

🔍 Advanced Troubleshooting

If emails still don't arrive, try these advanced steps:

Gmail-Specific Solutions:

  • Check Gmail's "All Mail" folder (not just Inbox)
  • Search for "Afterstate Events" in Gmail
  • Check Gmail's "Important" section
  • Look in Gmail's "Updates" or "Promotions" tabs

Email Provider Settings:

  • Check if Gmail has aggressive spam filtering
  • Verify email forwarding rules
  • Check if emails are being auto-deleted
  • Look for email size limits
