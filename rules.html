2. Privacy Policy

2.1 Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, purchase tickets, or contact us for support. This may include:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)
  • Event preferences and attendance history
  • Communications with our support team

2.2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process ticket purchases and send confirmations
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send important updates about events you've registered for
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

2.3 Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this policy. We may share information with trusted partners who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business.

2.4 Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

3. Event Guidelines

3.1 Age Requirements

Each event may have specific age requirements. Attendees must meet the minimum age requirement and may be asked to provide valid identification at the venue.

3.2 Dress Code

Events may specify dress codes. Attendees are expected to comply with these requirements. Entry may be refused for inappropriate attire.

3.3 Prohibited Items

The following items are generally prohibited at events:

  • Weapons of any kind
  • Illegal substances
  • Outside food and beverages (unless specified)
  • Professional recording equipment
  • Large bags or backpacks

3.4 Photography and Recording

Personal photography may be permitted, but professional recording equipment is prohibited unless authorized. Some events may be completely photography-free zones.

4. Ticket Policy

4.1 Ticket Validity

Tickets are valid only for the specific event, date, and time indicated. Each ticket admits one person unless otherwise specified.

4.2 Ticket Transfer

Tickets may be transferred to another person, but the original purchaser remains responsible for ensuring the transferee complies with all terms and conditions.

4.3 Lost or Damaged Tickets

Digital tickets can be re-sent to your email address. For physical tickets, replacement may be possible with proof of purchase and valid identification.

4.4 Duplicate Tickets

Each ticket has a unique QR code. Duplicate or fraudulent tickets will be denied entry, and no refund will be provided.

5. Refund Policy

5.1 Cancellation by Attendee

Tickets may be cancelled and refunded up to 24 hours before the event start time. A processing fee may apply. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

5.2 Event Cancellation

If an event is cancelled by the organizer, full refunds will be provided automatically. If an event is postponed, tickets remain valid for the new date, or refunds may be requested.

5.3 Weather and Force Majeure

Outdoor events may be subject to weather conditions. In case of severe weather or other force majeure events, refund policies will be communicated on a case-by-case basis.

5.4 No-Show Policy

No refunds are provided for no-shows or late arrivals after the event has begun.

6. Code of Conduct

6.1 Respectful Behavior

All attendees are expected to treat others with respect and courtesy. Harassment, discrimination, or disruptive behavior will not be tolerated.

6.2 Safety First

Attendees must follow all safety instructions and venue rules. Report any safety concerns to event staff immediately.

6.3 Alcohol and Substances

If alcohol is served, it will only be provided to attendees of legal drinking age with valid ID. Illegal substances are strictly prohibited.

6.4 Consequences

Violation of the code of conduct may result in removal from the event without refund and potential banning from future events.
